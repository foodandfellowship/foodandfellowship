Serving Zephyrhills with hope

Food, fellowship, and community every Sunday.

Food and Fellowship exists to serve people in Zephyrhills, Florida with a meal, encouragement, and a place to belong. We believe every person matters, every story matters, and hope still belongs in this community.

Weekly Community Meal

  • Where: Shepard's Park, Zephyrhills
  • When: Every Sunday
  • Time: 3:00 PM - 5:00 PM
  • Who: Everyone is welcome

Why we serve

Food and Fellowship is about more than handing out meals. It is about creating a place where people are seen, welcomed, encouraged, and reminded that they are not alone.

Feed people well

We provide meals to individuals and families in need and create space for people to gather with dignity.

Build real connection

We are here for conversation, prayer, encouragement, and community, not just quick transactions.

Strengthen Zephyrhills

We believe local outreach can bring hope, unity, and practical support right where people live.
