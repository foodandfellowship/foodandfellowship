Food and Fellowship

Serving the Zephyrhills Community Since 2016

Our Mission

Food and Fellowship is a grassroots outreach dedicated to serving individuals and families in need in the Zephyrhills community. Through weekly meals and fellowship, we provide food, encouragement, and connection for those experiencing hardship.

Sunday Community Meal

Location: Shepard's Park – Zephyrhills, Florida
Time: Sunday 3:00 PM – 5:00 PM

Everyone is welcome. Our goal is simple — share food, build relationships, and support one another.

Our Impact in Zephyrhills

Since 2016, Food and Fellowship has been serving the Zephyrhills community by providing meals and fellowship for individuals and families facing hardship.

  • Serving the community since 2016
  • Over 10,000 hours of community service
  • Thousands of meals shared with neighbors in need
  • Expanded outreach during COVID serving six days per week
  • Weekly community meals every Sunday at Shepard’s Park

Our mission is simple: bring people together, share a meal, and remind those who are struggling that they are not alone.
