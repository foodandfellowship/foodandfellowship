Serving the Zephyrhills community since 2016

Our Mission

Food and Fellowship is a grassroots community outreach serving individuals and families in need in Zephyrhills, Florida. Through meals, fellowship, and encouragement, we create a welcoming place where people can gather, be seen, and know they are not alone.

Our Impact in Zephyrhills

Since 2016, Food and Fellowship has continued to serve neighbors in need through consistent meal outreach and community support.

  • Serving the community since 2016
  • Over 10,000 hours of community service
  • Thousands of meals shared with neighbors in need
  • Expanded outreach during COVID, serving six days per week
  • Continuing weekly meals every Sunday at Shepard’s Park

Our mission is simple: share food, build relationships, and remind people that hope still has a place in this community.
